# 🏡 Sydney Real Estate Investment Analysis — Ryde LGA

An Excel-based real estate investment analysis for the **Ryde Local Government Area (LGA)** in Sydney, developed as part of a financial analytics assignment. The project uses historical housing price data adjusted for inflation to evaluate Ryde as a potential investment location and deliver a recommendation to a prospective client.

---

## 📋 Project Overview

Acting as an analyst for a financial advisory company specialising in Sydney real estate, this project examines median housing prices in Ryde from 1991 to 2021. All dollar amounts are adjusted for inflation using CPI data, and the analysis covers strata, non-strata, and all-dwelling categories separately.

---

## 🔍 Analysis Summary

**Advantages of investing in Ryde**
- Real housing values show a steady upward trend over time alongside Greater Sydney
- Nominal values exceeding real values in some periods indicate strong capital growth
- Strata and non-strata dwellings show notable price variation, offering diverse entry points

**Disadvantages of investing in Ryde**
- All-dwelling growth rate of 9.47% lags behind Greater Sydney's broader market
- Non-strata price volatility signals potential market turbulence
- Investors seeking maximum growth may find Greater Sydney more attractive overall

**Recommendation**
Investors valuing stability may find Ryde a reasonable choice, while those seeking higher growth potential may prefer the broader Greater Sydney market. Extensive research and professional consultation are advised before committing.

**2021 Reflection**
Median sales prices declined between 2017 and 2021, suggesting shifts in housing supply, demand, or broader economic conditions. By 2021, the median price for all dwellings in Ryde was **$1,080,000**, while non-strata properties commanded a significantly higher median of **$2,161,000**.
